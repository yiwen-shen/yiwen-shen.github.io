Welcome to my homepage!

I now work as an Assistant Professor at ISOM Department, HKUST. I graduated with a PhD degree from the Decision, Risk, and Operations Division at Columbia Business School. Before that, I graduated from Peking University with a bachelor degree in Physics, and Columbia Univeristy with a master degree in Financial Engineering. I have worked/interned in several financial firms as a quantitative analyst, including Morgan Stanley, Bank of America, and Cubist Strategiest.


See  Research  for my research work. My teaching experience can be found in Teaching.


I look forward to discussing intereting research questions and opportunities. I also welcome students with solid background and interests in research. Feel free to contact me by email: yiwenshen@ust.hk
